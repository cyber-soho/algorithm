Understanding Case Behaviors

An algorithm isn't just fast or slow. Its performance depends on the input. Input determines which personality (Best, Worst, or Expected) shows up.

Golden Rules of Performance

  • 1. Know your n: The same code can succeed or fail depending on data size.
  • 2. Choose specialized tools: Generic solutions often scale poorly.
  • 3. Think in growth: Ask "What happens if data increases 1000x?"
